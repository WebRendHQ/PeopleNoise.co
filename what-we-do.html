MARKETING FOR THOSE WHO WANT TO GET NOISY.

We are PEOPLENOISE Co,  a small marketing firm focused on events marketing and overall in-person engagement. Our goal is to bring people together in the most meaningful ways possible, all while creating vivid, extrodinary memories.

AIMING FOR EXCITEMENT, INSTEAD OF REVENUE.

At PEOPLENOISE, we aim for the excitement of the events we help promote, and not the overall revenue. We do this because we sell the feeling of excitement, instead of money-hungry, and that in turn will allow all of the attendees to attend, maybe even include a plus 1.

One day, I had the idea to start a party entertainment service. I wanted to show everyone that throwing an epic party could be done easily. I began hosting parties, drawing large crowds and making each event a huge success. However, as the number of attendees grew, it became challenging to manage everything alone.

That's when it hit me – why not help others who might struggle to gather such large crowds? I decided to offer my services to those who needed help promoting their events. I started reaching out to people, asking if they needed flyers, banners, and other promotional materials.

Over time, this side hustle evolved into a full-fledged business, which I named PeopleNoise. The idea was to create a platform that specialized in event promotion, helping others make noise about their parties and events. PeopleNoise became known for its creative and effective marketing strategies, ensuring every event was a memorable success.
